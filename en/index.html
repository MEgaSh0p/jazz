The Dracula Investigation

Discover the real life story of the legendary Vlad The Impaler

Join Us On An Epic Journey

This journey will lead you to the roots of Vlad the Impalers epic life story. You will learn all about the inspiration behind the famous vampire: Count Dracula. His story is so impressive that we think it deserves to be told on it’s own. 

The Ruler himself will take 20 minutes of his time to accompany you through five rooms filled with animations and sculptures.

Design

We teamed up with a young scenographer who helped us create the design from scratch. We knew that Romania is filled with young talented people waiting to be discovered and are so happy we met Silvia Ioana Horobeanu. Working with Silvia was an excellent choice! Together with her and many other young romanian artist’s varying from narators to sculptors we created The Dracula Investigation.
